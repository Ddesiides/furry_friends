About Me

Introducing Flora! Flora is a sweet Female who is about 4 years old. Flora is a gentle dog who loves attention! She loves being brushed and would make a great companion with other pets or kids. Flora would do well with any female dog. She doesn’t mind cats! She is house trained and walks well on leash. Flora can be afraid of men at first but warms up quickly. She will bark at strangers until she feels her warning is heard!

About Our Fostering Process

We want to make sure our furry friends are going to a good foster home. The foster fee is only $30 a month to provide any vet visits and supplies. Supplies will be delivered! Once welcomed to your home we will send monthly e-mails with a survey about your new friend or friends! You can choose to foster until you friend is adopted, or choose with the minimum of 6 weeks, if under six weeks there will be an additional fee.
